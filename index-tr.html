Türkiye'nin Öncü Bağımsız Yapay Zeka Araştırma Laboratuvarını Kuruyoruz

Titiz araştırmalar, işbirlikçi yenilikler ve insanlığın yararına sorumlu geliştirmeler yoluyla yapay zekanın sınırlarını zorluyoruz.

Aref.ai Hakkında

Türkiye merkezli bağımsız bir yapay zeka araştırma laboratuvarı olarak, bilimsel mükemmellik ve insan odaklı değerlere bağlılıkla, ticari baskılar olmadan YZ yenilikçiliğinin geliştiği bir gelecek yaratıyoruz.

Bağımsız Yapı

Bilimsel özgürlüğü ve operasyonel özerkliği sağlamak için tamamen bağımsız bir araştırma kurumu olarak organize edilmiştir.

Bilimsel Dürüstlük

Ticari etkilerden veya kısa vadeli kâr motivasyonlarından bağımsız, bilimsel merak ve toplumsal etki ile yönlendirilen araştırmalara odaklanma.

İnsan Odaklı YZ

Misyonumuz, insan yeteneklerini artıran, anlamlı zorlukları ele alan ve uzun vadeli insan refahına uygun yapay zeka sistemleri geliştirmektir.

Science is better when shared

As AI capabilities grow exponentially, the concentration of research in a handful of organizations creates bottlenecks for scientific progress. The research community needs independent spaces where creativity and scientific inquiry can flourish without corporate constraints.

To address this need, we've established Aref.ai as Türkiye's independent research laboratory focused on advancing foundational AI capabilities while maintaining complete scientific autonomy. We believe that transformative AI innovation requires both technical excellence and freedom to pursue long-term research directions.

AI that works for everyone

We're focused on the symbiosis between human and artificial intelligence. Rather than pursuing full autonomy, we're designing systems that amplify human creativity and problem-solving through intuitive, collaborative interfaces.

We envision AI that adapts to diverse human needs and contexts. While today's systems show remarkable capabilities in structured domains, we're developing technology that understands and enhances the full spectrum of human expertise across disciplines, cultures, and challenges.

Solid foundations matter

Foundational capabilities drive impact. While focusing on human-AI collaboration, we recognize that algorithmic intelligence itself remains fundamental. We're advancing core AI capabilities in domains like reasoning, learning, and planning—developing models that can enable transformative applications from scientific discovery to tackling climate challenges.

Research infrastructure excellence. Technical breakthroughs require exceptional tooling. We prioritize building robust, efficient, and secure infrastructure that accelerates innovation, enables reproducible research, and supports collaboration across research teams and with the broader scientific community.

Multimodal understanding and interaction. The future of AI requires systems that seamlessly integrate diverse forms of knowledge—from text and code to images, audio, and physical sensing. We're creating AI that understands and communicates through multiple modalities, making interaction more natural, expressive, and contextually aware.

Research Focus

Our research spans core AI methods, novel learning architectures, and responsible development practices—all aimed at creating technologies that enhance human capabilities and address meaningful challenges.

Foundation Models

Pioneering large-scale AI systems with reasoning, planning, and knowledge integration capabilities that push the boundaries of what's possible.

Learning Algorithms

Developing advanced learning techniques that improve data efficiency, transfer knowledge across domains, and adapt quickly to new challenges.

Human-AI Collaboration

Creating intuitive interfaces and interaction paradigms that enable seamless collaboration between humans and AI systems across diverse applications.
